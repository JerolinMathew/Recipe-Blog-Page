Creamy Garlic Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 120ml heavy cream
  • 40g grated parmesan

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
  2. Sauté garlic in oil until fragrant.
  3. Add cream and parmesan; toss pasta with sauce.
  4. Season and serve with parsley and lemon zest.

Blueberry Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup blueberries

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients; combine wet ingredients separately.
  2. Fold wet into dry and add blueberries.
  3. Cook on a griddle until golden and serve with syrup.
